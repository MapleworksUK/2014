Event - Business Information for SMEs

Thursday 23rd January, 6.00pm-8.00pm
We are hosting a free information session in conjunction with Kingston University Business School. The sessions will cover sources of funding and support services for SMEs offered by Kingston University or the government.

Programme

6.00pm: Registration and coffee.

Session 1 – ‘Knowledge, Skills and Funding for Innovation- What help is out there?’

Kathryn Elliott, Business Development Manager (Faculty Business and Law) at Kingston University
As UK business emerges from the recession, initiatives have been put in place to support SMEs and help them grow and develop. Kathryn will go through some of these initiatives, some offered specifically by Kingston University, others of which are government funded.

Session 2 - ‘Right-sized’ Learning Opportunities for SMEs

John Forgan, Director of Work Based Learning at Kingston University Business School
John will explain how the work-based learning team can work with local SMEs to devise a credit-based programme of study that allows progression in many practical business areas right up to master’s level.

No need to book. Just turn up at 73 Maple Rd, Surbiton KT6 4AG.
