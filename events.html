Events at Maple Works - 2014

Nigel Gilroy Business Start Up Club

Third Tuesday every month
Nigel's club is designed for any size of business that has started up within the last 2 years. Meetings are monthly on the third Tuesday of the month. Speakers, networking, workshops. Each session will have a theme/subject chosen by the club members. A portfolio of knowledge will be built based on the material of each sessions and be available as an online reference.

You can book the tickets via Eventbrite or pay at the event.

Kingston Business School - Information for SMEs

Thursday 23rd January, 6.00pm-8.00pm
We are hosting a free information session in conjunction with Kingston University Business School. The sessions will cover sources of funding and support services for SMEs offered by Kingston University or the U.K. government. The programme information and slides are available here.
