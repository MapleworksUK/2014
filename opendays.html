Open days

An Open Day is an invitation to explore Maple Works free for the day. Just walk in with your laptop and spend the day working merrily with unlimited coffee and internet. We will happily taken any queries for memberships and probably offer a special discounted rate valid only for that day.

Okay we'll make the offer even more tempting. Come in between 9:00 - 10:30 am and have a breakfast of pancakes or bacon rolls on the house. Tea or coffee as per your choice is available all day long.

Select a date and put it in your calendar - upcoming open days are Thursday 23rd and Friday 31st January 2014.

There is no need to book in advance. Just turn up at 73 Maple Rd, Surbiton KT6 4AG or call us for more information.
