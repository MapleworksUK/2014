Memebership options

If you have spent any time working at home or local cafe then this New Year is the best time to swap the noise & cold cup of coffee with a professional desk & productivity.

If you need access only during offpeak hours or weekends then we offer a special discounted price.

Call us today and book a free day to work with unlimited wifi and coffee. Alternately you can visit us on any Open Day. Come and join us in creating the best work place in outer London for connecting with local community and growing your business.
